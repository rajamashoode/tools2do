Pakistan Electricity Bill Check by Provider

Use this section to understand how duplicate bill checking works for major Pakistani electricity distribution companies. Each provider has its own service area, billing portal, and reference-number format.

HAZECO Online Bill Check

HAZECO serves consumers in the Hazara region. Before checking your duplicate bill, confirm that your reference number belongs to the Hazara service area and enter the complete number exactly as printed on your bill.

PESCO Online Bill Check

PESCO customers in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa can use their printed reference number to access duplicate bill information. If the bill does not appear, check that you selected the correct provider and current billing month.

LESCO Online Bill Check

LESCO covers Lahore and nearby regions. Users should verify the reference number from a previous bill and make sure there are no spaces or missing digits before checking the latest duplicate bill.

MEPCO Online Bill Check

MEPCO serves Multan and a large part of South Punjab. Enter the full reference number from your electricity bill to find the relevant duplicate bill through the correct provider source.

IESCO Online Bill Check

IESCO covers Islamabad, Rawalpindi, and nearby areas. If your bill is not showing, confirm the billing company and check whether the latest billing cycle has been uploaded by the provider.

FESCO Online Bill Check

FESCO customers in Faisalabad-region areas should use the exact bill reference number. This helps avoid errors caused by using an old or incomplete consumer number.

GEPCO Online Bill Check

GEPCO serves Gujranwala-region consumers. Always compare your entered reference number with an older printed bill before attempting duplicate bill lookup.

HESCO Online Bill Check

HESCO customers in Hyderabad and surrounding areas should confirm the correct distribution company and reference number format before checking bill details.

SEPCO Online Bill Check

SEPCO serves Sukkur-region consumers. If a bill is not available, the latest monthly bill may not yet be updated or the wrong provider may have been selected.

K-Electric Online Bill Check

K-Electric serves Karachi and surrounding areas and uses its own billing system. K-Electric customers should verify bills through the relevant K-Electric bill reference details rather than WAPDA distribution-company references.

Electricity Bill Checking FAQs

Can I check my electricity bill without a reference number?

Most providers require a valid reference number or consumer details. The safest method is to use the reference number printed on a previous bill.

Why is my latest electricity bill not showing?

The latest bill may not be uploaded yet, the wrong provider may be selected, or the reference number may be incomplete.
