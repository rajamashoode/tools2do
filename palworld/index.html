How Palworld Breeding Works

Breeding in Palworld is a core mechanic that allows players to produce offspring Pals with desirable stats and passive skills. Understanding the formula unlocks the ability to systematically create the most powerful Pals.

The Breeding Formula

Child Rank = Floor((Parent1 Rank + Parent2 Rank + 1) / 2)

Every Pal has a hardcoded "Breeding Power" (rank) that ranges from 10 to 1500. The child's rank is determined by averaging the two parent ranks and rounding down. The resulting rank then maps to the closest matching Pal.

Requirements to Breed

  • Unlock the Breeding Farm at Technology Level 19 (costs 100 Wood, 20 Stone, 50 Fiber)
  • Place one male and one female Pal in the Breeding Farm
  • Provide at least one Cake (requires Flour, Red Berries, Milk, Egg, Honey)
  • Wait for the egg to be laid, then use an Egg Incubator to hatch it

Passive Skill Inheritance

Each parent passes down up to 2 of their passive skills to the offspring. This makes breeding chains extremely powerful — you can stack the best skills like Legend, Musclehead, Ferocious, and Swift onto a single Pal through multiple generations.

Special (Unique) Breeding Combinations

Certain Pals can ONLY be obtained through specific parent combinations, bypassing the standard formula entirely. These are called "unique combos" — for example, Jormuntide Ignis can only be bred from Jormuntide + Relaxaurus Lux. Our calculator accounts for all special combinations.

Fusion Pals

Some special breeding combinations produce a "Lux" or alternative variant Pal. These fusion Pals are often stronger than the base version and have unique Partner Skills. Notable fusions include: Mossanda Lux, Grizzbolt (from Mossanda + Grizzbolt), and Jormuntide Ignis.
