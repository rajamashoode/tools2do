What is the Benazir Income Support Programme (BISP) 2026?

The Benazir Income Support Programme (BISP) is the flagship poverty alleviation initiative by the Government of Pakistan. Previously associated with the Ehsaas Program during the previous administration, the core focus remains the same: providing unconditional cash transfers to the most vulnerable, low-income households across Pakistan.

For the fiscal year 2026, the government has significantly increased the social protection budget. The primary **Benazir Kafaalat Program** stipend has been officially raised to **Rs. 14,500 per quarter** to help citizens combat rising inflation and utility costs.

How to Check 8171 Balance Online via Web Portal

Checking your balance has never been easier. The government has phased out older tracking systems (like the Ehsaas tracking pass gov pk) and consolidated everything into the new 8171 web portal. Follow these exact steps:

  1. Click the "Check BISP Status Online" button at the top of this page.
  2. You will be redirected to `https://8171.bisp.gov.pk/`.
  3. Enter your 13-digit CNIC number in the designated box. Do not use spaces or dashes.
  4. Enter the Captcha code (the numbers shown in the image) for security verification.
  5. Click "Submit" to view your current Kafaalat balance, Taleemi Wazaif status, and eligibility score.

How to Check BISP Registration via 8171 SMS

If you do not have access to the internet or a smartphone, you can use the offline SMS method. This is a free service provided by the government.

  • Open the messaging app on your mobile phone.
  • Type your CNIC number.
  • Send the message to the shortcode 8171.
  • Within 5 to 10 minutes, you will receive an official reply in Roman Urdu detailing whether your funds have been transferred to your designated bank account or HBL Konnect agent.

BISP Taleemi Wazaif: Education Stipend 2026

The **BISP Taleemi Wazaif** program is a conditional cash transfer designed to encourage education and reduce dropout rates among children of BISP beneficiaries. To qualify, a child must maintain a minimum of 70% attendance in a government-recognized school. The government provides a higher stipend for female students to promote girls' education.

The NSER Dynamic Registry (New Registration)

In previous years, BISP relied on a static door-to-door National Socio-Economic Registry (NSER) survey. If a family was missed, they could not receive funds. In 2026, the government has fully transitioned to the **BISP Dynamic Registry**.

If your 8171 status shows as "Na-Ahal" (Ineligible), or if your financial situation has worsened recently, you can actively update your poverty score. There are over 600 BISP Tehsil offices across Pakistan where Dynamic Registry desks are operational.

Required Documents for NSER Survey

  • Original Computerized National Identity Card (CNIC) of the female head of the household.
  • Updated B-Form (Bay-Form) issued by NADRA for all children.
  • A registered mobile SIM card in the name of the applicant.
  • Latest electricity or gas bill (if applicable).

🚨 Fraud Alert & Scam Prevention

BISP officially communicates ONLY through the 8171 shortcode. If you receive a WhatsApp message, a phone call from a standard 11-digit mobile number (e.g., 0300-XXXXXXX), or an SMS from any code other than 8171 claiming you have won a cash prize or Ehsaas funds, it is a scam. Never share your CNIC, ATM PIN, or bank details with these fraudulent callers.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Below are the most commonly searched questions regarding the BISP 8171 program in Pakistan:

Is the Ehsaas Program 25000 still active in 2026?

No. The Ehsaas 25,000 cash grant was an emergency relief program deployed during the severe floods and the COVID-19 pandemic. The permanent, ongoing social safety net is the BISP Kafaalat program, which currently distributes Rs. 14,500 quarterly.

Why did my BISP payment stop?

Payments are typically halted if a family's socioeconomic status improves (e.g., someone gets a government job, travels abroad, or buys a vehicle), or if the beneficiary fails to update their NADRA records. You must visit the Dynamic Registry desk to recertify your status.

What is the BISP Nashonuma Program?

Nashonuma is a specialized health and nutrition initiative under BISP. It provides additional cash stipends and specialized nutritious food to pregnant and lactating women, as well as children under the age of two, to prevent stunting and malnutrition.

Can men apply for the BISP Kafaalat program?

The BISP Kafaalat stipend is strictly issued to the female head of the eligible household. This policy ensures the financial empowerment of women and guarantees that the funds are utilized for the family's welfare and children's education.
