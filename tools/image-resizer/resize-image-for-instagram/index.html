What is the Resize Image for Instagram?

The Resize Image for Instagram helps you quickly create an image with the right dimensions for Instagram posts and social media images. Instead of opening heavy editing software, you can upload a picture, enter the width and height, resize it in your browser and download the result.

How to use this image resizer

  1. Select your image file.
  2. Enter the required width and height in pixels.
  3. Click Resize Image.
  4. Download the resized image and use it where needed.

Recommended guidance

For Instagram, square 1080 × 1080 images are widely used, while portrait and landscape posts may use different dimensions. Choose the format that fits your post design.

Common use cases

  • Preparing images for social media profiles and posts.
  • Creating smaller images for websites and blogs.
  • Making photos fit online application or document requirements.
  • Reducing oversized images before uploading.
