What is the Word Counter?

The Word Counter on Tools2Do is a free online utility made for quick, simple and practical work. It helps you count words, characters, sentences and paragraphs instantly, without installing extra software or creating an account.

This page is designed as a complete tool page, not just a small widget. You can use the tool above, read the steps below, check common use cases and open related tools from the same category.

How to use this tool

  1. Paste or type your text into the box above.
  2. Click Count Words to see your stats instantly.
  3. Review words, characters, sentences and paragraphs.
  4. Click Clear to reset and start again.
