What is the Twitter/X Character Counter?

The Twitter/X Character Counter helps you check the exact length of your Twitter/X post text before you publish, submit or send it. Instead of guessing whether your text is too short or too long, you can paste it into the box above and instantly see character count, word count, spaces and line count.

This pSEO page is focused on one specific job: twitter character counter. That makes it more useful than a general counter because the guidance, examples and related links are written for this exact use case.

How to use the Twitter/X Character Counter

  1. Paste your text into the box above.
  2. Click Count Now to see characters, words, spaces and lines.
  3. Compare your result with the guidance below.
  4. Edit your text and count again until it fits your target length.

Twitter/X post length guide

Twitter/X posts are short-form by nature, so every word matters. This counter helps you trim text, check spacing and make your message readable before posting.

Use the counter to keep posts focused, especially when adding links, hashtags, mentions or emoji. Shorter posts are often easier to read and share.
