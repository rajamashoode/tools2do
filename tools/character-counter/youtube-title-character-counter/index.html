YouTube title length guide

YouTube titles should be clear, searchable and easy to read on mobile. A title that is too long may be cut off in search results, suggested videos or mobile screens.

Keep the main keyword near the start, avoid unnecessary filler words and count before publishing. A concise title can improve clarity and click-through rate.
