Frequently asked questions

What is the LinkedIn post character limit?

LinkedIn feed posts can contain up to 3,000 characters. The first ~700 characters are visible before readers click "see more", so the opening of your post matters most.

Does LinkedIn count spaces and line breaks?

Yes. LinkedIn counts all characters including spaces, line breaks and emoji. Emoji may count as 1–2 characters depending on the character.

What is the LinkedIn headline character limit?

Your LinkedIn headline can be up to 220 characters. Most users see only the first 60–90 characters in search results and profile cards, so lead with the most important information.
